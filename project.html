  4. Hospital Management System

    My groupmates and I are currently developing a hospital management system. Patients can book appointments, select doctors and preferred dates. The system also includes other user roles such as Doctor, Nurse, and Admin. Doctors can accept or cancel appointments, write prescriptions, and view consultation history. Nurses can view appointments and write reports. Admins have the ability to add, edit, and remove staff information in the system.
